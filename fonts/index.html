Want To Be A Member

Follow these instructions

If you want to be member of The Mission Happiness Team:

  1. Submit the google form at the bottom of the page:
  2. OR Mail the following details to: missionhappiness65@yahoo.com
    1. Name
    2. Father name
    3. Mother name
    4. Gender
    5. Date of birth
    6. Address
    7. City
    8. District and State
    9. Mobile no.
    10. Email id
    11. Reference
  3. If you have been referred by an existing member, write his/her Name and number. Else, write how you come to know about the organization.
  4. The confirmation mail will be sent back to applicant within 24 hrs. If they don't get confirmation mail within 24hrs, they can call on helpline no. - 07307474043
  5. In confirmation mail we will direct you about the membership criteria and donation criteria. Applicant will become Registered member when donation will received as per donation criteria.
  6. Once we receive your donation the receipt will be mailed to you within 48 hrs. Please keep it for future reference.
  7. If you do not receive receipt. Mail your complaint on our email id.
